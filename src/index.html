The Organization

Philippine Cubers Association reappeared on national television on June 28, 2007 to promote the association and announced the upcoming Rubik’s Cube Philippine Open 2007, the first speedcubing competition in the country which was recognized by WCA. When the first Philippine Open was finalized, it was then scheduled to take place in August 25, 2007. With its success, it gathered huge attention to cubing enthusiasts and then PCA has begun growing exponentially.
